About Westpoint

Founded on traditional sporting values, WestPoint is in it to win it for all our customers. We offer Developer augmentation, Serverless software development, Identity & Access management solutions, Microservices & big data.
We build solid, full time teams in-house to build and deliver your project, or, we offer skilled AWS team members to augment your existing team. We build to deliver.

Our Philosophy

We listen and we learn. One of the greatest skills we have is the ability to truly listen to what our existing or potential customers are facing in regards to tech solutions or challenges. Taking time to understand the needs of our clients has always been at the centre of our philosophy at WestPoint. This allows us to deliver exceptional service and products to our customers.

Our Goals

Build

Build a company with great values, relationships and reputation.

Grow

Ensure that all our team members have opportunities to grow as people and for WestPoint to grow alongside our customers!.

Deliver

Deliver Excellence, always.
